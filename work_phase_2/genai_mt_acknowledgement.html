It talks about GenAI

GenAI is a project that aims to create a machine translation system that can translate text from one language to another with high accuracy and speed. The project is being developed by a team of researchers and engineers at GenAI Inc., a leading artificial intelligence company. The project is funded by the National Science Foundation and has received support from the Department of Defense and other government agencies.
