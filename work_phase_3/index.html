Welcome to the Style Guide for our proposed website, created as part of Work Phase 3. This site serves as a comprehensive reference for the visual and interactive elements that will be used in the final project. Here, you will find our colour palette, typography choices, spacing guidelines, and reusable UI components ‚Äì all designed to ensure a consistent and accessible user experience.

Community Focus Area: Youth Mental Health

The focus of our project is Youth Mental Health. Our aim is to create a supportive, informative, and engaging digital space for young people, their families, and the wider community. By providing reliable resources, sharing personal stories, and connecting users with local support services, we hope to break down stigma and empower young people to seek help when needed.

Our Aims

  • Design a visually appealing, accessible, and user-friendly website.
  • Promote awareness and understanding of youth mental health challenges.
  • Offer easy access to trusted resources and community support services.
  • Foster a sense of connection and belonging among young people.
  • Ensure consistency and professionalism through a robust style guide.
