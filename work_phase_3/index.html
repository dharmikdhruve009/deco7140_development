About This Site

This site is deisgned for aiding new people who have just come or about come to Australia for further living.It is designed to be a resource for young people, their families, and the wider community, focusing on youth mental health. The site will provide information, support, and resources to help young people navigate their mental health journeys. Students will be able to access information about mental health challenges, local support services, and personal stories from presenters and other young people.

Site Focus Area

The focus of our project is Youth Mental Health. Our aim is to create a supportive, informative, and engaging digital space for young people, their families, and the wider community. By providing reliable resources, sharing personal stories, and connecting users with local support services, we hope to break down stigma and empower young people to seek help when needed.
