Community Stories

Real experiences from people around the world who made Australia their new home for study, work, or family. Read their journeys, challenges, and advice for newcomers.

Anna Müller

🎓 Germany ✈ Cairns, QLD | Master’s Student

“Moving to Queensland for my Master’s was daunting, but the international student community helped me settle in. The resources and events made it easy to make friends and succeed academically. My advice: join a study group early!”

Rajesh Kumar

PhD Candidate | India ✈ Brisbane

“Arriving with my family for a PhD was a big step. The university’s support for partners and kids made our transition smooth. My children love their new school, and my research group is truly international.”

Lina Torres

Skilled Migrant | Colombia ✈ Gold Coast

“I came to Australia for work. Finding a community of expats and locals helped me feel at home. If you’re new, don’t hesitate to reach out—Australians are welcoming, and there’s always support available.”

Paul Cook

Skilled Migrant | London ✈ old Coast

“I came to Australia for my job in medicine. Being introvert whole life made me feel lonely despite coming to an English speaklimg country. This is where I was able to open up and I now enjoy meeting over sports and also learnt surfing.
My Advice: Be active over your favourite communities and use the website to build a better you.”
